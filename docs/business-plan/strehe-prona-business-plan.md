# Strehe-Prona Business Plan

## Business Overview

- Business name: Strehe-Prona
- Location: Prishtina, Kosovo
- Business type: Property services company
- Stage: Pre-launch / startup

Strehe-Prona is a startup property services company based in Prishtina, Kosovo. The company will provide recurring and on-demand property support services for owners and landlords, with a focus on inspections, cleaning coordination, maintenance coordination, repairs management, handovers, and key management.

The company has already built its own internal operations platform. This software is not the product sold to customers. It is an internal business tool used to manage contracts, task scheduling, service packages, billing, expenses, key tracking, and reporting. This gives Strehe-Prona better control, lower administrative overhead, and stronger service organization than a traditional small service business.

The business will begin with a lean operating model. The founders will manage customer communication, scheduling, supervision, reporting, and simple low-complexity work directly. Specialist work will be outsourced to trusted contractors and technical partners. This allows the company to keep fixed costs low while still offering a broad service range.

Strehe-Prona has access to 100 m2 of mixed-use premises in Prishtina, available free of charge for 2 years, which significantly lowers startup costs and risk.

The company is seeking startup financing of EUR 12,000 to support initial setup, equipment, secure key and document storage, marketing, and working capital.

## Problem and Opportunity

Many property owners need reliable, organized, and responsive support for ongoing property-related tasks, but they do not want the burden of managing every inspection, cleaning issue, repair, handover, or key-related process themselves.

Strehe-Prona will act as a trusted operational partner for property owners by delivering recurring support services and coordinating execution efficiently. The business is designed as a service company first. Technology supports execution, internal control, and scale, but revenue comes from services delivered to customers.

## Services

The initial service offer will include:

- Property inspections
- Cleaning coordination
- Basic maintenance handling
- Repair coordination
- Handovers
- Key management and key custody
- Recurring property service packages
- One-off service requests

Service delivery model:

- Founders handle coordination and simple low-complexity tasks
- Specialist work is outsourced to contractors
- The company earns direct service revenue and coordination margins on managed jobs

For more complex technical work, Strehe-Prona will manage the customer relationship, scheduling, oversight, and service flow, while partner contractors perform the specialist execution.

## Target Customers

The main target customers are:

- Property owners
- Landlords
- Owners with multiple units or properties
- Agencies or intermediaries managing owner relationships
- Clients who need reliable recurring property support

The strongest early fit is with customers who want peace of mind, one contact point, recurring property oversight, organized handling of small issues before they become larger problems, and basic reporting around service delivery.

## Competitive Advantage

Strehe-Prona's main advantages are:

- Internal software already built for operations
- Lean low-cost startup model
- Free mixed-use premises for 2 years
- Flexible contractor-supported delivery
- Ability to track contracts, tasks, billing, and keys in one system
- Ability to create repeatable service packages instead of only one-off jobs

This gives the company better operational control than many traditional informal service providers.

## Business Model

The company will generate income from two main sources:

Recurring revenue:

- Monthly or recurring service packages for property support

Non-recurring revenue:

- One-time jobs
- Handovers
- Inspections
- Repair coordination
- Contractor-managed projects with margin

For specialist work:

- Contractor performs the service
- Strehe-Prona manages the process
- Strehe-Prona earns approximately 10-20% coordination margin, depending on the job

This blended model reduces fixed staffing needs and supports gradual scaling.

## Operations Plan

The company will operate from a 100 m2 mixed-use base in Prishtina. The premises will be used for administration, storage, equipment organization, secure key and document management, and operational coordination.

Initial team:

- Founder 1: Operations, service structure, workflow management, customer coordination
- Ermal (co-owner): Marketing, media, visual communication, customer-facing promotion
- Accounting support available through close family support
- Contractors and partners for specialist services

The business will start lean, without building a large employee base immediately.

## Marketing and Sales

Early marketing channels will include:

- Website
- Social media campaigns
- Visual content and promotional material
- Personal outreach
- Referrals
- Local trust-building and word-of-mouth

The company's early positioning should focus on reliability, responsiveness, professional coordination, simple communication, and recurring care rather than only emergency fixes.

The goal is to secure the first 10-20 active customers and then grow toward 30-40 customers while keeping quality controlled.

## Funding Need

Strehe-Prona is seeking EUR 12,000 in startup financing to become operational and cover early-stage setup plus working capital.

The company is a suitable candidate for a modest startup loan because:

- Startup costs are controlled
- Premises are already secured
- Founders are actively involved
- The model is service-based and practical
- Specialist work can be scaled through contractors rather than fixed payroll

## Risks and Mitigation

Main risks:

- Slow early customer acquisition
- Unpredictable job volume in the first months
- Cash flow pressure from delayed payments
- Dependence on reliable contractors for specialist work

Mitigation:

- Low fixed overhead
- Free premises for 2 years
- Founder-led execution
- Lean operating model
- Recurring package strategy
- Use of contractors instead of full-time technical staffing

## Conclusion

Strehe-Prona is positioned as a lean, operationally disciplined property services startup in Prishtina. The company combines practical field service coordination with internal software-driven control, allowing it to deliver services more efficiently than traditional small operators. With modest startup funding, secured premises, and a low-overhead model, the business has a realistic path to launch and grow through recurring customer relationships.
