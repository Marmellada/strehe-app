## 8. Strategic pricing warning

Two mistakes are possible.

### Mistake 1: pricing too high too early

Risk:

- low conversion
- too much explanation needed
- audience feels the service is too premium before trust is built

### Mistake 2: pricing too low

Risk:

- the business gets busy without becoming healthy
- founder burns out
- staff later becomes hard to support

The goal is not cheapest.

The goal is:

- believable
- sustainable
- defendable
