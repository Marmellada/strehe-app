# STREHË Survey Testing Plan

## Purpose

This document defines a short survey for testing STREHË with potential customers when interviews are not practical.

The goal is to collect useful signals about:

- whether the problem is real
- whether the packages are understandable
- whether the prices feel believable
- what trust proof people need
- whether people would contact STREHË

The survey should take:

- 5 to 7 minutes

---

## 1. Survey rules

Keep the survey:

- short
- specific
- easy to answer on phone
- direct about pricing
- direct about trust concerns

Do not ask:

- “Do you like this idea?”

Better questions are:

- “What would stop you from using this?”
- “Which package would you consider?”
- “Which price feels too high?”
- “What would you need to trust this?”

---

## 2. Who should receive it

Best respondents:

- Kosovo diaspora living abroad
- people who own apartments in Kosovo
- people whose family owns apartments in Kosovo
- people who know diaspora apartment owners
- spouses/family members who influence decisions

Target:

- first 20 to 50 responses if possible

Even 10 honest responses are useful.

---

## 3. Survey intro text

Use this at the top of the form:

```text
We are testing a new service for Kosovo diaspora apartment owners.

STREHË would help owners who live abroad by checking and caring for apartments in Prishtina and Fushë Kosovë, sending clear updates, and helping with local follow-up when needed.

This survey takes about 5 minutes. Your answers help us understand whether the service, packages, and prices make sense.
```

## 12. How to read results

Do not only look at average answers.

Look for repeated patterns.

## Strong positive signs

- people recognize the problem
- Care Plus is understandable
- WhatsApp is preferred
- photo reports matter
- key handling comes up often but feels solvable
- some people want a trial visit

## Warning signs

- people think this is just cleaning
- people do not understand the package differences
- most people say pricing is too expensive
- people do not trust key handling
- people assume repairs/cleaning are included

---

## 13. Decision table after responses

| Result pattern | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Essential is popular but Care Plus feels high | strengthen value of Care Plus or adjust pricing |
| Many want trial visit | create a paid trial visit offer |
| Most ask about keys | add key/access explanation to website |
| Many think cleaning is included | make boundaries clearer |
| Arrival Ready is confusing | rename or reposition it |
| People prefer WhatsApp | keep WhatsApp-first CTA |

---

## 14. Recommended first response target

First target:

- 20 responses

Minimum useful signal:

- 10 honest responses

Better signal:

- 30 to 50 responses

Do not wait for perfect data.

Use the survey to improve the offer, not to delay launch forever.

---

## 15. Working summary

This survey should answer:

- is the problem real?
- are the packages clear?
- are the prices believable?
- what trust proof is needed?
- would people contact through WhatsApp?
- should STREHË offer a paid trial visit?

The most important result is not praise.

The most important result is knowing what must be clearer before launch.
